The common system pattern

These projects are not random demos. They follow the same architecture: use data to forecast pressure, apply rules to prevent unsafe recommendations, let agents explain/co-ordinate, and keep the human decision-maker in control.

📥
Operational inputPatient, staffing, supply, or workload signals
📈
ML predictionXGBoost / forecasting model estimates pressure
🛡️
Rules + guardrailsSafety, escalation, and business constraints
🤖
Command agentsExplain, coordinate, document, and audit
👤
Human approvalNurse, manager, or operator confirms decision

Portfolio architecture

The same blueprint can power healthcare and workforce applications: a model predicts pressure, deterministic logic enforces safety, agents explain the recommendation, and the final decision is recorded.
